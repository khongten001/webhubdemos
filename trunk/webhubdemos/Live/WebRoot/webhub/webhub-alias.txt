Please define an alias, or "virtual path", in your web server software such that

	http://localhost/webhub/index.html

brings up the index.html document in this directory.

and

	http://localhost/webhub/demos/c/whdemos.css

brings up a style sheet (no 404 error).


If necessary, replace "localhost" with your domain name or IP number, and port. 

Example:

	http://192.168.0.15:8000/webhub/index.htm
